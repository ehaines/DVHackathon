Browser Safety Tips

How to search the internet safely.

It is common for an abuser to try to control your access to information by checking your computer and phone to see where you have been on the Internet. You can protect yourself by keeping your Web browser from storing any of this information or by erasing it.

To keep your browser from storing the information, you can put it into private or incognito mode. Here is how you do that for different browsers:

  • IE 9 – choose Incognito Browsing from the Safety menu (AltS)
  • IE 8 – choose In Private Browsing from the Safety menu or (CtrlShiftP)
  • Mozilla Firefox – from the menu choose New Private Window and then type in your URL (Web address). From a link, right click on the link and choose Open Link in New Private Window.
  • Chrome – from menu select New Incognito Window or push CtrlShiftN.

If you have already been searching the Web, you can clear your search history by pushing CtrlShiftDelete in Windows or CommandShiftDelete in its (MAC).
